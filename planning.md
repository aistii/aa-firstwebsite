# Website Styling
This file includes information on element styling, including colors and fonts.

## Text and Logo Styling
### Logo Title
- __Font:__ Bebas Neue (capitalized sans-serif, narrow)
- __Font Color:__ #f2f2f2 (light gray)
### H1 Headings
- __Font:__ Barlow (italicized, bold) (Barlow: SemiBold 600 Italic)
- __Font Color:__ Dependent on background color:
	- #a63117 (brick red) on lighter background (subject to change)
	- #f2f2f2 (light gray) on darker background (subject to change)
### H2 and Smaller Headings:
- __Font:__ Barlow (normal weight) (Barlow: Regular 400)
- __Font Color:__ Dependent on background color:
	- #734d45 (chocolate color) on lighter background (subject to change)
	- #f2f2f2 (light gray) on darker background (subject to change)
### Paragraph and Other Small Text:
- Same styling as H2 and smaller headings.

## Website Main Elements
- __Primary Background Color:__ #f2f2f2 (light gray)
- __Section Accent Color:__ #f2a679 (light orange)
- __Small Accent Color:__ #d9564a (pink, but not exactly _pink_ pink)
- __Navigation Bar Color:__ #a63117 (brick red)
	- __Navigation Bar Text Color:__ #f2f2f2 (light gray)
	- __Navigation Bar Font:__ Barlow (heavier weight to better see)
